Promise of Sharing Research Data

  • Digital data boom
  • New push to share and conduct research in the open
  • Share the wealth

Current Status

  • Researchers are not directly incentivized to share
  • Sharing is time consuming and an unfamliar practice
  • There are ethical challenges to sharing
  • It's not clear what sharing entails

Curation As a Service

The challenges of curation

Active Curation

  • Allows researchers to curate their own data
  • Interfaces and language that are familiar
  • Upload As You Go
  • Makes sharing a button press at the end of a study

Lessons Learned

  • Pay attention to how researchers work
  • Make the process of sharing dovetail with current workflows
  • It's a significant shift for a community of practice
  • Provide incentives
